About me

I am a certified executive coach and expert in the area of leadership development. My approach is a relentless commitment to organizational growth and executive excellence among my clients and colleagues around the globe.

Over the last two decades I’ve been successfully supporting companies and leaders in achieving their ambitious goals and sustaining results. My offer includes executive coaching, mentoring, and leadership programs. My intercultural competencies, strong leadership skills, excellent coaching and mentoring abilities, unique expertise in instructional design of

training programs allow me to provide additional value for multinational ventures. I specialize in consulting global companies on the issues of human resources’ development and design original training programs for corporate teams. I also act as a personal coach for leaders and managers of companies that have vast geography.

My list of corporate clients includes numerous Fortune 1000 companies.
I hold two master's degrees from Columbia University. The latest one was gained in 2004 in organizational psychology and leadership.
In 2005 I completed professional coach training at Erickson College International and in 2006 I was certified as an Executive Coach by Korn Ferry.
I also have 10 years of managerial experience gained while working in the US.
