My approach

Coaches are frequently asked a question: what’s your signature style? Defining mine I came up with 4 essential qualities that create my unique flavor.

  1. As a devoted practitioner of adaptive coaching, I tune my style with my coachee’s state to be in sync. It requires on my part intuition, precision and courage to decide on an approach to meet my coachee’s current need whatever it might be: support, challenge, wit, pause, space, encouragement, faith etc. This method enables my clients to achieve instant results with poise and confidence.
  2. I have been a seasoned champion of “edutainment” long before it became mainstream. As much as coaching results matter, defining a unique road that to them is of a paramount importance for me. Thus, I am constantly in search of innovative ways of engaging my clients into a captivating learning journey, aiming at creating meaningful, memorable and profound experiences for them.

My task is to elevate their self-awareness to the level of inspiration so they would summon the will, brace for challenge and start exploring wonderful future for themselves.
