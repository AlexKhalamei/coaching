Coaching

As an executive coach I adhere to the notion that even our most successful clients still have a lot of potential. I am proud to accompany them in these journeys of personal discovery.
For me, executive coaching is always a partnership. The foundation of this partnership is a mutual trust. I trust my clients to be committed to excellence and high performance, to learning and growth. They trust me to be competent, loyal, and a reliable partner; who listen, observe, and support them.

I challenge my clients to acknowledge their blind spots, which requires a great deal of courage and humility on their part. I also encourage them to explore their strengths and talents so they feel confident to move on and excel in even the most turbulent environment.

Common Focus Areas for Coaching:

Managing vision and purpose

Motivating your team

Leadership style

Career path

Conflict management

Drive for high performance

Leading a complex business challenge
